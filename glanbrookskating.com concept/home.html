About Glanbrook Skating Club

The Glanbrook Skating Club is a non-profit organization, recognized by Skate Canada. We are proud to offer high quality skating programs to skaters of all skill levels in the Community of Binbrook/Glanbrook.

Our executive members are all volunteers dedicated to the operations of the club and are elected at an annual general meeting held each spring. We always need more volunteers; simply reach out at glanbrookskating@outlook.com, or attend our next general meeting.

All our programs are taught by Skate Canada NCCP Certified Professional Coaches, and run from fall to early spring.
